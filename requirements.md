# DR eTicket Requirements

Travellers must provide accurate information when completing the DR eTicket. Preparing the required details in advance helps ensure a smooth process.

Official reference:  
https://dominicanrepubliceticket.us/

## Information You Need

- Valid passport  
- Flight number and airline  
- Travel dates (arrival and departure)  
- Accommodation address in the Dominican Republic  
- Personal contact information  
- Customs declarations  
- Basic health information  

## Who Must Complete the eTicket?

- All international visitors  
- Dominican citizens returning home  
- Residents leaving or entering the country  
- Families (one form can include multiple members)

## When to Complete It

- Before boarding your flight  
- For both arrival and departure  
- Ideally 24–72 hours before travel

The DR eTicket ensures efficient processing at Dominican Republic airports and replaces all previous paper forms.
