# DR eTicket Overview

The **DR eTicket** is the official digital entry and exit form required for all travellers visiting or leaving the Dominican Republic. It combines immigration, customs, and health information into a single online document.

Travellers must complete the eTicket before boarding their flight. Airlines and border authorities may request proof of completion during check-in or upon arrival.

Official reference:  
https://dominicanrepubliceticket.us/

## Key Points

- Mandatory for all travellers (arrival and departure)
- Fully digital — no paper forms required
- Generates a QR code after completion
- Must be completed separately for inbound and outbound travel
- One form can include multiple family members

The eTicket improves border efficiency and ensures accurate traveller information for Dominican Republic authorities.
