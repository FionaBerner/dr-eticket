# How to Complete the DR eTicket

Completing the DR eTicket involves entering travel, passport, and personal information into the official digital form. The process is straightforward when the required details are prepared in advance.

Official reference:  
https://dominicanrepubliceticket.us/

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Access the official eTicket platform**  
   Use a secure internet connection and ensure your passport is available.

2. **Select travel direction**  
   Choose whether you are completing the form for arrival, departure, or both.

3. **Enter personal information**  
   Provide your full name, date of birth, nationality, and passport details.

4. **Add travel details**  
   Include flight number, airline, accommodation address, and travel dates.

5. **Complete customs and health sections**  
   Answer standard questions related to items carried and health status.

6. **Review all information**  
   Ensure accuracy to avoid delays during travel.

7. **Submit the form**  
   After submission, a QR code will be generated.

8. **Save the QR code**  
   Keep a digital or printed copy for airline and border checks.

## Tips for a Smooth Process

- Use the same passport you will travel with  
- Double-check flight numbers and dates  
- Complete the form at least 24–72 hours before travel  
- Ensure all family members are included if travelling together
