# 🇩🇴 DR eTicket — Dominican Republic Digital Travel Form

This repository contains structured documentation and informational content about the **DR eTicket**, the mandatory digital form required for all travellers entering or leaving the Dominican Republic. The goal is to provide clear, accurate, and accessible guidance for completing the eTicket process.

Official reference website:  
https://dominicanrepubliceticket.us/

---

## 📘 What Is the DR eTicket?

The DR eTicket is a unified digital form that replaces the previous paper-based immigration, customs, and health declarations. All travellers—tourists and residents—must complete the eTicket before arriving in or departing from the Dominican Republic.

This repository provides:

- A general overview of the DR eTicket  
- Step-by-step instructions for completing the form  
- Requirements and information travellers need to prepare  
- Common issues and troubleshooting guidance
